Discord Pick-up Game Bot

pug-bot is a Discord bot used for organizing pick-up games. It's programmed entirely in Python 3.


The bot makes it easy to visualize the teams: everyone can see who's on each team and which players who haven't been picked yet. It also automatically moves the teams to separate voice channels as soon the the PUG is started.


Its other features include randomizing teams and captains, custom lobby names and sizes, custom team names, and picking voice channels for a team.
